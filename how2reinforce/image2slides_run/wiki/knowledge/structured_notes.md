# Structured Notes

## Source
- `raw.pdf` is the only knowledge base for this rerun.
- 77 lung cancer fecal samples underwent shotgun metagenomic sequencing.
- 52 samples with PD-L1 TPS were grouped as TPS<1% (n=17), TPS 1-49% (n=18), TPS>=50% (n=17).
- Analyses include diversity, composition, differential taxa, co-occurrence network, ARGs, microbiome-ARG association, and functional pathways.

## Figure-first policy
- Keep exact source panels only for data/result figures.
- Internal text in diagrams/icons should be converted to editable slide text or omitted.
- Each result slide uses one main figure whenever possible; two panels only when the pair is needed to read one result.
- Text is limited to a conclusion sentence plus a reading cue.
